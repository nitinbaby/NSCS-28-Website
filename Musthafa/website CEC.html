Update February 10, 2021: CEC/ICMC 2021 is going Virtual!

CEC/ICMC’21 has cancelled its face-to-face conference scheduled for September 12-16 in Honolulu.
The new virtual conference dates are July 19 – 23, 2021.
The website will be updated with new information within the next week. Please be sure to check back.
Site last updated: February 10, 2021

This site will be the source of all conference related information and is being updated frequently. Please be sure to check back often!

Please add the cecicmc@centennialconferences.com email address to your address book to ensure that all announcements and future correspondence is received.
